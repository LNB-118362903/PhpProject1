Ultimate Frisbee

How to Play

Ultimate (normal "Frisbee" is dropped due to it being copyrighted) is 7 aside outdoor sport. The aim of the game is to pass the disc from person to person and into the end zone while the other team is on defence. The match starts off with a pull, this is where you throw the disc to other team. When you are holding the disc you are not aloud to move. You are aloud use one foot as a pivot foot to step around the defender marking you. If a pass is incomplete then the other team gets the disc. When one team scores the other team goes to the opposite end and the team that scored throws the disc back to them to restart the match.

Rules

Travelling - Any movement of your pivot foot while attempting a throw is considered a foul, if called the disc is given to other team.

Contact - Ultimate is strictly a non-contact sport, therefore contact should be kept to a minimum

Counted Out - While attempting to get off a throw your defender will be counting to 10. If they reach 10 and you haven't throw the disc, the other team gets the disc

Fast Count - If your count is considered too fast you can call a fast count where you have to slow down your count

Double Marking - If 2 people or more are marking the same man that is a foul.
